# Component Constraints

## Must Always Do
<<<EXAMPLE OPTIONS:
1. Preserve original ordering of inputs.
2. Surface unknown or missing data explicitly.
3. Remain deterministic given the same inputs.
>>>

## Must Never Do
<<<EXAMPLE OPTIONS:
1. Guess or infer missing values.
2. Modify source data.
3. Reach outside its defined responsibility.
>>>

## Explicit Non-Goals
<<<EXAMPLE OPTIONS:
1. Performance optimization beyond correctness.
2. Cross-component orchestration.
3. User-facing presentation logic.
>>>
